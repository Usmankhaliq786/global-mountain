Pakistan's Most Trusted
Mountain Travel Partner

Global Mountain is a premier travel agency dedicated to curating extraordinary mountain adventures across Pakistan and the world. Founded with a passion for exploration, we have been transforming the way people experience nature's most breathtaking landscapes.

From the majestic peaks of Naran and Skardu to the lush valleys of Swat and Hunza, we bring you closer to Pakistan's unmatched natural beauty — safely, affordably and memorably. Our team of experienced travel specialists handles every detail so you can focus on the journey.

  • Customized packages for families, couples, groups and solo travelers
  • Best-price guarantee on flights, hotels and complete tour packages
  • 24/7 customer support before, during and after your journey
  • Offices in both Islamabad and Karachi for your convenience
